Features

Everything You Need to Download

Professional-grade tools without the price tag. Built for macOS power users who demand reliability and speed.

4K & 8K Video Download

Download at the highest available resolution, up to 8K. Video and audio streams merged seamlessly via FFmpeg.

Audio-Only Extraction

Extract high-quality M4A or MP3 audio from any video. Perfect for music, lectures, and podcasts.

Browser Cookie Auth

Auto-extracts login cookies from Safari, Chrome, Firefox, or Arc. Access age-restricted content with no manual steps.

Live Download Progress

Real-time progress bar, speed, and ETA with live terminal output — you always know what's happening.

Playlist & Channel Support

Download entire playlists or channels in one click. Files are automatically organized with index numbers.

Native Folder Picker

Choose any destination via macOS native Finder dialog. Your folder preference persists across sessions.

Format Fallback Engine

When YouTube changes its API, the app automatically retries alternative format chains — 99% success rate.

Cancel & Clear History

Cancel any download instantly. Clear history from the UI without deleting files from your disk.

100% Private

Zero analytics, zero telemetry. Everything runs locally on your Mac. Nothing ever leaves your device.

How It Works

Up and Running in Seconds

No configuration needed. Paste, pick your quality, and download.

1

Paste Your URL

Copy any YouTube or supported site URL. Single videos, full playlists, and entire channels all work.

2

Choose Quality

Select Best, 720p, or Audio-Only. Pick MP4 or M4A/MP3 output. Optionally choose a browser for cookie auth.

3

Click Download

Hit Download Now and watch the live progress bar. Files appear in your chosen folder when complete.

4

Open & Enjoy

Click Open on any file to launch it directly in QuickTime, Music, or your preferred player.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before downloading.

Is YT Downloader really free? +
Yes. 100% free and open-source under the MIT license. No trials, no paywalls, no hidden fees — ever.
Does it work on Apple Silicon Macs? +
Yes. The app is compiled as a native arm64 binary, optimized for M1/M2/M3/M4. It also runs on Intel Macs.
How do I download age-restricted videos? +
Select your logged-in browser (Safari, Chrome, Firefox, or Arc) in the app. Cookies are extracted automatically — no manual export needed.
Why does macOS say "unidentified developer"? +
The app is not yet notarized (planned for v2.0). Right-click the app → "Open" → "Open" in the dialog. You only need to do this once.
Can I download entire YouTube playlists? +
Yes. Paste a playlist URL and the app downloads all videos sequentially, organized with index numbers automatically.
What sites besides YouTube are supported? +
Over 1000 sites including Vimeo, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, Reddit, Twitch, Coursera, Udemy, and many more.
Is my data safe? +
Absolutely. YT Downloader has zero telemetry, zero analytics, and zero crash reporting. Everything stays on your device.
