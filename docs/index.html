How It Works

Download in 3 Simple Steps

No configuration needed. Paste, pick quality, and download.

1

Paste Your URL

Copy any YouTube (or 1000+ sites) URL and paste it into the input field. Single videos, playlists, and channels all work.

2

Choose Quality & Format

Select Best, 720p, or Audio-Only. Choose your output format (MP4 or M4A/MP3). Optionally pick a browser for cookie auth.

3

Click Download

Hit "Download Now" and watch the live progress. Files appear instantly in your chosen folder when complete.

4

Open & Enjoy

Click "Open" on any downloaded file to launch it directly in QuickTime, Music, or your preferred player.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before downloading.

Is YT Downloader really free? +
Yes, completely. 100% free and open-source under the MIT license. No trials, no paywalls, no hidden fees — ever.
Does it work on Apple Silicon Macs (M1/M2/M3)? +
Yes. The app is compiled as a native arm64 binary, optimized for Apple Silicon. It also runs on Intel Macs.
How do I download age-restricted videos? +
Select your logged-in browser (Safari, Chrome, Firefox, or Arc) in the app. YT Downloader extracts cookies automatically — no manual export needed.
Why does macOS say "unidentified developer"? +
The app is not notarized yet (planned for v2.0). To open it: right-click → "Open" → "Open" in the dialog. You only need to do this once.
Can I download entire YouTube playlists? +
Yes. Paste a playlist URL and the app downloads all videos sequentially, organized with index numbers automatically.
What sites besides YouTube are supported? +
Over 1000 sites including Vimeo, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, Reddit, Twitch, and educational platforms like Coursera and Udemy.
